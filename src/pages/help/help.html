Help Profile Page

Introduction page where you can edit your personal information by clicking on pencil icon in the top right hand corner.

Portfolio Page

The portfolio page allows the patient to import documents on the phone or by taking an image with the camera.

You also can search by name or filter by type and date of the documents.

You can also email selected documents.



Diary Page

The diary allows for a patient to record information about themselves. It has almost the same functionality as the portfolio page in terms of adding documents. It is to record anything you wish to report to your medical advisor on your next visit.

The edit and delete are handled by the icons in the top right hand corner of the entry.

Reminder Page

The reminder page allows the user to create the following:

When to take your medication X times per day.

When is your next appointment with your medical advisor.

The user will be notified even when not in the application.
